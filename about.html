WHO ARE WE?

Printu is a start up business with the initiative of collegiate students from the Technological University of the Philippines Visayas located in Talisay City, Province of Negros Occidental. This line of business is intended for us to give convenience and access to students of TUP-V for accessible printing within the school premises, providing a cloud-based storage of files in our personalized web application. Our price ranges will be dependent also with the competition of the market concerning the printing services outside the campus such as printing house and internet cafes.
